## Overview

The Framer integration brings AI-powered image generation directly into your Framer design workflow. Create stunning visuals, illustrations, and graphics without leaving your favorite design tool.

## Key Features

- **Direct Integration**: Generate images within Framer using PowerAI's powerful engine
- **Smart Templates**: AI-suggested templates based on your design context
- **Version Control**: Track and manage different image variations
- **Export Options**: Download in multiple formats optimized for web
- **Collaboration**: Share generated assets with your team in real-time

## How It Works

1. **Install the Plugin**: Add the PowerAI plugin to your Framer project
2. **Select an Element**: Choose any image or container in your design
3. **Generate Content**: Use AI prompts to create custom visuals
4. **Refine and Edit**: Adjust parameters and regenerate as needed
5. **Export and Deploy**: Use your generated images directly in your site

## Use Cases

- **Hero Sections**: Create impactful hero images and backgrounds
- **Illustrations**: Generate custom illustrations for landing pages
- **Product Mockups**: Produce product showcases and promotional graphics
- **Blog Thumbnails**: Design engaging thumbnails for blog posts
- **Icon Sets**: Create consistent icon families for your brand

## Setup Instructions

To set up the Framer integration:

1. Open your Framer project
2. Navigate to the Plugins marketplace
3. Search for "PowerAI" and install
4. Connect your PowerAI account
5. Configure your preferred settings and style presets
6. Start generating images directly in your designs

## Technical Details

- **Plugin Version**: v2.1.0
- **API Integration**: RESTful API with WebSocket support
- **Supported Formats**: PNG, JPG, SVG, WebP
- **Max Resolution**: Up to 4096x4096 pixels
- **Processing Time**: 2-10 seconds depending on complexity

## Pricing

The Framer plugin is free to install. Image generation uses your PowerAI quota. Pro plans include advanced features like style transfer and batch processing.
