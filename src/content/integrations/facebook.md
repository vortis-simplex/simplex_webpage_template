## Overview

The Facebook integration enables you to automatically generate and publish AI-powered images to your Facebook pages and groups. Streamline your social media marketing by creating engaging visual content without manual design work.

## Key Features

- **Automated Posting**: Schedule and auto-post generated images to your Facebook pages
- **Group Management**: Share content across multiple Facebook groups simultaneously
- **Ad Creative Generation**: Create compelling ad images for Facebook campaigns
- **Story & Reel Support**: Generate vertical content optimized for Stories and Reels
- **Analytics Integration**: Track engagement metrics for AI-generated content

## How It Works

1. **Connect Your Account**: Link your Facebook business or personal account
2. **Select Pages/Groups**: Choose which destinations to post to
3. **Set Templates**: Create reusable image templates with your branding
4. **Schedule Content**: Plan posts in advance or trigger them automatically
5. **Monitor Performance**: Track engagement and optimize your strategy

## Use Cases

- **Social Media Marketing**: Generate promotional images, product showcases, and event graphics
- **Brand Consistency**: Maintain visual identity across all Facebook content
- **A/B Testing**: Create multiple image variations to test performance
- **Content Scaling**: Produce large volumes of visual content efficiently
- **Community Building**: Engage followers with fresh, relevant imagery

## Setup Instructions

To set up the Facebook integration:

1. Go to PowerAI Integrations → Facebook
2. Click "Connect" and authenticate with Facebook
3. Grant permissions for page and group access
4. Configure posting preferences and scheduling
5. Set up content templates and branding guidelines
6. Test with a sample post

## Technical Details

- **API Version**: Facebook Graph API v18.0
- **Authentication**: OAuth 2.0 with page access tokens
- **Supported Content**: Posts, Stories, Reels, Ads
- **Image Formats**: JPG, PNG (optimized for Facebook)
- **Resolution**: 1080x1080 (square), 1200x630 (landscape), 1080x1920 (portrait)

## Pricing

Facebook integration is included in all plans. Usage counts toward your monthly quota. Business and Enterprise plans include advanced features like bulk posting and enhanced analytics.
