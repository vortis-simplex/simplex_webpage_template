### Job Description

We are seeking an experienced Product Manager to lead the development of our innovative products. The ideal candidate will have a strong product sense, excellent communication skills, and the ability to translate customer needs into successful product features. You will work closely with engineering, design, and marketing teams to deliver products that delight users.

### Responsibilities

- Define product vision, strategy, and roadmap aligned with business goals
- Gather and prioritize requirements from customers, stakeholders, and market research
- Create detailed product specifications and user stories
- Collaborate with engineering and design teams to deliver high-quality products
- Conduct user research and analyze usage data to inform product decisions
- Manage product lifecycle from concept to launch and beyond
- Monitor product performance and iterate based on feedback and metrics
- Communicate product updates and progress to stakeholders across the organization

### Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Business, Engineering, or a related field
- 5+ years of experience in product management
- Proven track record of launching successful products
- Strong understanding of product development methodologies (Agile, Scrum)
- Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
- Ability to balance user needs with business objectives
- Strong communication and presentation skills
- Experience with product management tools (Jira, Confluence, or similar)

### Benefits

- Competitive salary with performance-based bonuses
- Equity participation in the company
- Comprehensive health and wellness benefits
- Flexible work arrangements and remote options
- Professional development budget
- Regular team events and company retreats
- Opportunity to work on cutting-edge products
