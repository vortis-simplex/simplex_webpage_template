### Job Description

We are looking for a skilled DevOps Engineer to join our infrastructure team. The ideal candidate will have extensive experience in cloud infrastructure, automation, and CI/CD pipelines. You will be responsible for building and maintaining the systems that enable our development teams to deliver high-quality software efficiently.

### Responsibilities

- Design, implement, and maintain cloud infrastructure on AWS, GCP, or Azure
- Build and optimize CI/CD pipelines for automated testing and deployment
- Monitor system performance and implement improvements for scalability and reliability
- Automate infrastructure provisioning and configuration management
- Implement security best practices and ensure compliance with industry standards
- Troubleshoot and resolve infrastructure issues in production environments
- Collaborate with development teams to improve deployment processes and reduce lead times
- Document infrastructure architecture and operational procedures

### Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field
- 4+ years of experience in DevOps or infrastructure engineering
- Strong knowledge of containerization technologies (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Experience with infrastructure as code tools (Terraform, CloudFormation)
- Proficiency in scripting languages (Python, Bash, or similar)
- Experience with CI/CD tools (Jenkins, GitLab CI, GitHub Actions)
- Understanding of networking, security, and cloud architecture principles
- Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work under pressure

- Comprehensive health benefits package
- Flexible work schedule and remote work options
- Annual learning and conference budget
- Cutting-edge technology stack
- Collaborative and innovative work culture
