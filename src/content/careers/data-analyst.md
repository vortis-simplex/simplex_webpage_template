### Job Description

We are seeking a detail-oriented Data Analyst to join our analytics team. The ideal candidate will have strong analytical skills and experience in transforming raw data into actionable insights. You will work closely with various teams to support data-driven decision making and help optimize business processes.

### Responsibilities

- Collect, clean, and analyze large datasets from multiple sources
- Develop and maintain dashboards and reports to track key performance indicators
- Identify trends, patterns, and insights to support business decisions
- Create data visualizations and present findings to stakeholders
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define data requirements
- Develop and implement data quality checks and validation processes
- Document analysis methodologies and ensure reproducibility
- Stay updated on data analysis tools and best practices

### Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, or a related field
- 2+ years of experience in data analysis or business intelligence
- Proficiency in SQL for data extraction and manipulation
- Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, or similar)
- Knowledge of programming languages such as Python or R for data analysis
- Strong understanding of statistical concepts and methods
- Excellent problem-solving and critical thinking skills
- Ability to communicate complex findings to non-technical stakeholders

### Benefits

- Competitive salary and annual bonus
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Flexible remote work policy
- Professional development budget for certifications and courses
- Access to cutting-edge data tools and platforms
- Regular team collaboration and knowledge sharing sessions
